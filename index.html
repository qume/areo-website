AreoHawk Desktop

AreoHawk desktop software is a full photogrammetry suite for Windows. Highly scalable to thousands of images, whether low resolution from a drone or ultra high resolution from pro cameras. Turn image collections into single, cohesive tile sets and 3D DEM/DSM/point cloud datasets.

New for July 2014 - AreoHawk Personal Edition - Free!

We're delighted to announce the first full photogrammetry package available completely for free. Restricted to non commercial use, and maximum of 50 images.

AreoHawk Personal

Never before has aerial imaging been more accesible. From a GoPro on a kite, to an octocopter, high quality aerial imagery is available to everyone.

AreoHawk Personal allows you to take a bundle of images, even with different exposure levels, and create both beautiful maps and professional quality 3D terrain and model data.

Orthographic Maps

The higher quality the input images, the better the output, however the software does a supurb job of adjusting for various image problems to create a consistent, seamless map.

Input images need to have overlap between them, so the software can build a full 3d model to use to reproject the photos on the new map.

Even if the input images are in various directions or just all over the place, you'll be amazed at the quality of the map produced.

Flight Visualization Tools

A wide variety of tools are available to visualize the input data in various ways. This allows you to understand what happened in a flight, and optimize your capture strategy.

Mine Surveying

Quick turnaround 3D of open pit mines allow rapid quantative surveying.

Mine Surveying

Drones allow for a safe and quick turnaround to obtain 3D survey grade data over an entire mine in a matter of hours. No existing method can provide this level of survey data in such a short time.

City Modelling

Utilize Esri, LidarAnalyst, and a range of other tools to extract meaningful data from this versatile 3d data.
